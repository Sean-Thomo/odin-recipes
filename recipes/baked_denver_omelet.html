Baked Denver Omelet

Description

Denver omelet, also called western omelet, an omelet with ham, onion, and bell pepper; cheese is sometimes included.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • ½ green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped cooked ham
  • 8 eggs
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
